GUACAMOLE MEXICANO

Ingredients

  • 9 avocados
  • 3 limes/lemons
  • handful fresh coriander, chopped
  • salt and black pepper
  • chilli flakes to taste

Preparation

  1. cut the avos in half, take out seed. put the inside of avos in a morter.
  2. put in the lime juice
  3. smash the avos up, leaving some texture
  4. mix in the coriander, salt and pepper and make it as spicy as you'd like.
