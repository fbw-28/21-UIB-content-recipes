This is a receipt of the Oh-My-God!-tasty Spring Onion Bread!

Ingredients

  • Flour x 700 g
  • Spring onion x 1 bunch
  • Butter x 40 g
  • Egg x 1
  • Sugar x 15 g
  • Water x 20 g
  • Salt x 10 g
  • Some Pepper

Preparation

  1. Chop the spring onions.
  2. Mix the flour with egg, butter, water, yeast, salt, and sugar.
  3. Divide the dough in to 30-g units, roll them into balls.
  4. Let them sit for 1 hour.
  5. Let them sit for 1 hour.
  6. Put the chopped spring onions on top of each the dough ball.
  7. Let them sit for 30 minutes.
  8. Bake under 200 degree heat for 20 minutes.
